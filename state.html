Location:

  • The GPGP is located between Hawaii and California, within the North Pacific Gyre.

Composition:

  • It's primarily composed of plastic, with a significant portion being microplastics.

Size and Weight:

  • The GPGP is estimated to be larger than the size of Texas, containing millions of tons of debris.

Impact:

  • This accumulation poses a threat to marine life, as they can mistake plastic for food or become entangled in it.

Formation:

  • The GPGP is formed by the convergence of ocean currents, which carry plastic and other debris from various sources
